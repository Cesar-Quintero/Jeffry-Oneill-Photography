I'm Jeffry Oneill

Thank you for checking out my photography portfolio. I believe that art is in the process, not necessarily in the product, so please feel free to use my art in whatever way you feel drawn. Each of the photographs featured in this collection were an experience and experiment of creation with the tools and media - mirrorless cameras and Photoshop - available to me at the time. These works are unnamed, saved for numbers, and are open to interpretation.

I invite you to explore these works, see if they stir up any thoughts or feelings, and welcome any comment or story you may with to share via the contact form. Originals are available for purchase where available.
